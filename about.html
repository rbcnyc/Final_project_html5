About us

Exhausted was started in 2013 at London tech-hub GeneralAssemb.ly. We are designed to remove the worst polluting cars, buses and trucks from our neighbourhoods. To stay in touch:

Why?

London has a serious air pollution problem. Don't believe me? The EU is suing the UK for the level of harmful particles in our air. This hurts our friends, children, and anyone playing or doing exercise outdoors.

Why target cars?

Actually we target all vehicles as they are one of the largest, and most spread out sources of pollutants. If a vehicle can't filter visible toxins it certainly isn't filtering microscopic toxins, which cause permantent damange to people's lungs.

How to help?

We need you to report violating cars to the City of London and TFL - we make it dead easy. If you are interested we also need help promoting, developing and designing the website and especially contacting our local MPs. Submit your email address and we will be in touch.
